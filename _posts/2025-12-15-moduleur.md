Moduleur is fully analog, DIY-friendly modular synthesizer system based on the circuit blocks of the original [Shmøergh Hog](/synths/hog). The instrument is built from discrete modules — oscillators, filters, VCAs, utilities, and a digital “Brain” module — that can be used in a pre-patched configuration or integrated into any Eurorack system.

[Build guide](https://www.shmoergh.com/guides/moduleur-build-guide/) with lot's details on Shmoergh website.
