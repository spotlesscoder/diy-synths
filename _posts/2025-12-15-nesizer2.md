The 2A03 IC consists of a 6502 CPU core (with some minor changes), a DMA controller and the [Audio Processing Unit](https://www.nesdev.org/wiki/APU). The APU is controlled via 22 registers, which are connected internally to the 6502 only; there are no external input pins facilitating communication with the APU directly.
